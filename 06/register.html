Why we need to be safe?

Equal Rights Campaign is committed to providing a safe environment for all its members free from discrimination on any ground and from harassment at any place including sexual harassment. Pro Campaign Against Harassment will operate a zero tolerance policy for any form of sexual harassment anywhere, treat all incidents seriously and promptly investigate all allegations of sexual harassment. Any person found to have sexually harassed another will be reported to the authorities immediately. All complaints of sexual harassment will be taken seriously and treated with respect and in confidence. No one will be victimised for making such a complaint.
